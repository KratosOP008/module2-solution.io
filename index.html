Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish that has gained worldwide recognition. It is also known as "Murgh Makhani" in Hindi. The dish is made with tender pieces of chicken that are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and various spices, then cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce.

Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy Indian dish that features paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a luxurious sauce made with a blend of cashews, cream, and aromatic spices. "Shahi" translates to "royal," indicating the regal and indulgent nature of this dish. It is a favorite in many North Indian restaurants and homes.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish that consists of a thin, crispy crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with a flavorful and spiced potato filling. It is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, a lentil-based vegetable stew. Masala dosa is a beloved breakfast item but is also enjoyed as a snack or even a meal at any time of the day.
